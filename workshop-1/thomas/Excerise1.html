Thomas J.Watson Sr.

Thomas John Watson Sr. was born in February 17,1879, Campbell, New York, U.S. He contributed a lot in the world of technology and is a renowned known name in the field of technology. He was the chairperson and founder of IBM (International Business Machines): IBM is a prominent technology corporation company known all around the world for its inventions and achievements. Mr. Watson Sr. was of the leading self-made tycoons; He was also one of the most affluent men of his time. He was also Well Known for his exceptional sales skill; being called "The World's Greatest salesman". He was even present during World War 2 and personally met Adolf Hitler himself; IBM was also used during World War 2 by both US government and Nazi German for camp's operation and record keeping. He quoted a lot of remarkable quotes; one of them being -"To be successful, you have to have your heart in your business and your business in your heart".
